Storyline

A religion-based autocracy has taken over most of the United States, renaming the country Gilead.

In this country women are second-class citizens. Anyone trying to escape is punished. One such person is June, who is captured while trying to escape with her husband and child and is sentenced to be a handmaid, bearing children for childless government officials. As a handmaid, June is renamed Offred.

This is her story.
